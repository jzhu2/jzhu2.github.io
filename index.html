About Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a classic cartoon of a cat named Tom and a mouse named Jerry trying to outwit each other. There are many episodes of this cartoon, with the majority of them coming from 1940-1967. The last episode to date was made in 2005. For most of these episodes, these two characters don't talk but has nice sound effects to go along with the episode.

Episodes

In nearly every episode, Tom attempts to harm Jerry in any way, Jerry will usually find some way around it to harm Tom instead. For this episode, Tom tries to use Jerry as fish bait. However, with the intelligence and creativity of Jerry, he would trick Tom into reeling in objects other than fish.

More characters

This episode displays another character found in most of Tom and Jerry episodes. The big dog in the center is named Spike. He usually gets mad and tries to harm Tom when he tries to harm or annoy him in any way. However, this episode is unique where Spike, Tom, and Jerry all try to get along.
